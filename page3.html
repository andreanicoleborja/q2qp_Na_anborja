ABOUT US

Founded in 1961, the World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF) stands as a global beacon in environmental conservation, championing the cause of preserving the Earth's biodiversity and nurturing a symbiotic relationship between humanity and the natural world. Operating in over 100 countries, WWF's unwavering commitment is guided by a set of core values—integrity, respect, accountability, collaboration, and innovation—ensuring that its initiatives not only have a profound impact but also adhere to ethical principles. WWF adopts a holistic approach to conservation, addressing interconnected challenges such as deforestation, overfishing, climate change, and biodiversity loss through innovative solutions that span wildlife conservation, habitat protection, climate action, and sustainable development.

With a vision to build a future where humans live in harmony with nature, WWF envisions a world where biodiversity thrives, ecosystems are resilient, and humanity coexists sustainably within the planet's ecological boundaries. By fostering international cooperation, engaging with governments, businesses, and local communities, and promoting awareness and education, WWF strives to create a collective impact that transcends borders. The organization encourages individuals and organizations to join its mission, fostering a legacy of environmental stewardship and contributing to a more sustainable and resilient planet.

Mission

WWF's mission is to build a future in which humans live in harmony with nature. The organization is dedicated to addressing the most pressing environmental challenges and working towards a world where biodiversity thrives, ecosystems are resilient, and humanity lives sustainably within the planet's ecological boundaries. Through its diverse initiatives, WWF strives to preserve the Earth's natural heritage for future generations.

Vission

WWF envisions a world where biodiversity is flourishing, ecosystems are robust, and the delicate balance of nature is maintained. The organization aims for a future where humans coexist with the natural world, recognizing the interconnectedness of all living beings and the importance of a healthy planet. WWF's vision extends to creating a sustainable and harmonious relationship between humanity and the Earth, fostering a legacy of environmental stewardship.

Get Involved!

WWF invites individuals and organizations to join in its mission. Whether through donations, advocacy, or lifestyle changes, everyone is encouraged to be a part of the movement towards a more sustainable and nature-friendly future. By harnessing the collective power of people worldwide, WWF believes that positive change is achievable for the benefit of the Earth and all its inhabitants.
